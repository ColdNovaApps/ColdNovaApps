Privacy Policy

Coldnova ("we", "our", or "us") respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how our iOS applications handle information when you use them.

Information We Collect

We do not collect, store, transmit, or share any personal data. Our app does not collect names, email addresses, device identifiers, location data, usage analytics, or any other personal information.

On-Device Functionality

The app allows you to save birthdays and get notified about them. entirely on your device and are never sent to any server.

Get notified

The device can send you updates about birthdays. This action is processed locally on your device and does not transmit or log any data.

Third-Party Services

We do not use third-party analytics, advertising SDKs, tracking tools, or crash reporting services.

Subscriptions & Payments

The app offers optional subscriptions. All payments are processed securely by Apple through the App Store. We do not receive or store payment, billing, or financial information.

Children’s Privacy

We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under the age of 13. Because no data is collected, there is no risk of child data being stored or shared.

Data Security

Since we do not collect or store personal data, no user data is held on external servers.

Your Privacy Rights

Because the app does not collect personal data, there is no information to access, modify, or delete.
