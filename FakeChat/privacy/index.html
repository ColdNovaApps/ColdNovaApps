Privacy Policy

Coldnova ("we", "our", or "us") respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how the Fake Chat iOS application handles information when you use our app.

Information We Collect

Fake Chat allows users to enter custom text for notification titles and descriptions. All entered content is processed entirely on your device and is never collected, stored, transmitted, or shared by us.

We do not collect personal data such as names, email addresses, phone numbers, contacts, device identifiers, location data, analytics, or usage tracking.

App Functionality

Fake Chat generates custom notifications designed to visually resemble standard Messages app notifications. The app does not read, access, intercept, or interact with any real messages, SMS, iMessage, or other messaging applications.

On-Device Processing

All features of Fake Chat operate fully on-device. No data is sent to external servers, and no user-generated content ever leaves your device.

Third-Party Services

We do not use third-party analytics services, advertising SDKs, tracking technologies, or crash reporting tools.

Subscriptions & Payments

Fake Chat offers subscription-based features. All payments are securely processed by Apple through the App Store using Apple’s in-app purchase system. We do not receive, store, or have access to your payment or billing information.

Children’s Privacy

Fake Chat does not knowingly collect personal information from children under the age of 13. Since no personal data is collected, there is no risk of child data being stored or shared.

Data Security

Because Fake Chat does not collect or store any user data, no personal information is held on external servers, reducing privacy and security risks.

Your Privacy Rights

As no personal data is collected, there is no user data to access, modify, or delete.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated effective date.
